# Code Of Conduct

PayRails contributors should work in a professional, respectful, and
constructive way.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and kind when giving feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Assume payroll, banking, privacy, and compliance concerns deserve careful
  treatment.
- Welcome contributors with different levels of payroll, banking, software, and
  open-source experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private payroll, bank, tax, identity, or credential data.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to make unsafe compliance claims.
- Repeatedly derailing technical or project discussions.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block accounts, or take other
reasonable action to protect the project and its contributors.
